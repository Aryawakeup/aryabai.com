Who I Am

Yunan Bai — friends call her Arya.

Originally from Xiamen, a small island city in southeast China, now based in Amsterdam. Currently enrolled in the Research Master’s programme in Social Sciences at the University of Amsterdam, with a focus on quantitative methodology, and working as an intern at Elsevier.

Raised by a loving family, together with a cat who is 17 years old now. Childhood dream was to be a dragon slayer; in the end she grew up to be an indie game lover and TRPG nerd.

Besides all this, also an urban nomad — travelling, searching, learning across different cities and cultures. Full of love for the ever-changing world, exploring in the flow.
