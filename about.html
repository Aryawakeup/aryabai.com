Who I Am

Welcome to my corner of the digital world.

I'm a social science researcher interested in observing the world through the cracks of culture and structure. I'm currently pursuing a research-based master's degree at the University of Amsterdam, focusing on the intersection of political sociology and data methodology.

Algorithms and labor — analysing how digital platforms reshape the logic of work and exploitation.

Quantitative social analysis — using Python, R and Stata for statistical modelling, text analysis, and network analysis.

In addition to academics, I'm also a digital nomad and a traveler. This blog will occasionally publish research, thoughts, fragmented records, or analysis written late at night.

If you are interested in collaboration, research, projects, writing or anything interesting, please contact me.
